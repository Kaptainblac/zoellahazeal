When/Why Hire an Interior Designer

We suggest hiring a designer before or around the time of hiring an architect, builder or general contractor. An interior designer is able to enhance the project and ensure that you receive the end result that is in your dreams

Why Choose Us?

Right from the idea, we are with you all through the way down to the actualisation. You entrust your empty spaces to us, which to us we consider blank art convas. We employ our expertise, craftsmanship and creativity, we hand over ravishing work of art.

With a designer on board, you will have someone taking charge of all the decisions, someone who knows exactly how all of the pieces in your very large project puzzle impacts the next and someone who can and will take all this pieces and create a home that is breathtaking in every way

Budget

A budget is a map that shows us how we can do the best job for you. Our goal is to work within your investment range with a reasonable scope of work

Time

While we ensure we are precise and thorough in our delivery, we ensure we deliver within the set forth time for your convinience
